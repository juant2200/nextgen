Editor's Note

What the Numbers Don't Show

The headline numbers look healthy, but the real story is in deal structure. We're seeing more bridge rounds, more SAFEs where priced rounds used to happen, and founders accepting longer runways in exchange for control. That's discipline — and it's probably where we should all be right now.

The LatAm story continues to impress. Deal count is up, check sizes are maturing, and the operators being funded now are a different caliber than three years ago.
